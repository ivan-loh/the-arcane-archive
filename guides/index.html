Strategy & theorycraft guides

Long-form articles for theorycrafters, optimizers, and players who want to understand why a build wins, not just which one. Numbers throughout come from the reverse-engineered mechanics spec; where confidence is lower, callouts say so.

If you read one guide, read this one

Damage Optimization is the centerpiece reference for the whole wiki — it walks the full damage formula, ranks every relevant stat, defines six build archetypes, and works through four numerical examples. Most of the other guides reference it.

Suggested reading order

  1. Damage Optimization — the formula and the stat hierarchy. The foundation.
  2. Element Pairing — which infusions actually matter once you understand the formula.
  3. Spell Tier List — how every spell maps onto archetypes from the optimization guide.
  4. Artifact Priorities — what to grab when, given which archetype you’re committing to.
