Artifact priorities

The 20 in-run artifacts in pickup-priority order. The rankings are contextual — an artifact’s value depends entirely on the archetype you’re building. Read the archetype section of the damage guide first; this page assumes you know which one you’re committed to.

Where the numbers come from

All parameter values quoted here come from data/artifacts.json as extracted from Artifact_*.asset files. The interpretation of Parameters[] indices is inferred from the corresponding ArtifactStatModifier_* class names in the IL2CPP dump; some confidence labels apply.

Pickup order by archetype

Crit Stacker

  1. #17 HealOnCrit — turns offence into sustain; 0.3 HP per crit at high crit rate is sufficient self-heal.
  2. #7 CritDamage Standing Still — +100 CritDamage conditional. Functionally a 2× CritDamage doubler when you can stand still (Slash, Bell, Boomerang at apex).
  3. #14 CritDamage / Revive — +35 per revive performed. In a 4-player run you’ll hit 4–6 revives easily — +140 to +210 CritDamage.
  4. #20 CritDamage / Common — +1 per common item picked. Easy to stack +30–50 over a run.
  5. #5 Damage / Nearby Teammate — co-op only; +20% Damage per nearby ally.

Element Specialist

  1. #15 ElementalDmg / Infusion — the keystone. Take it the moment it appears. +10 ElementStrength per infusion performed; with 4+ infusions you’re multiplying everything by 1.40×.
  2. #5 Damage / Nearby Teammate — pure damage stack, additive but reliable.
  3. #16 FireRate Standing Still — cadence stacking is multiplicative against your inflated damage.
  4. #20 CritDamage / Common — passive late-game scaling.

Glass Cannon

  1. #1 LessHP / MoveSpeed — +21 MS at minimum HP; survival via mobility.
  2. #3 LessHP / XPGain — +40% XP at low HP; compounds into more shop picks, which compound into damage.
  3. #6 HealOnDodge — +20 HP per dodge. Pairs with low max HP to give a meaningful instant-heal.
  4. Avoid: #10, #18, #9 — max HP raises ruin the trade.

Tank-DPS

  1. #13 Damage / Armor — the keystone. +0.25 Damage per Armor means 100 Armor = +25% Damage in the bracket.
  2. #2 Armor / Level — feeds #13. +1 Armor per party-member level; at lvl 30 in a 4-stack that’s 120 Armor.
  3. #12 Armor / Objective — +10 Armor per side objective. 5 objectives = +50 Armor = +12.5% Damage.
  4. #11 Damage / Revive — complements the armor stack with a flat +10% Damage per revive.

Swarm Clearer

  1. #5 Damage / Nearby Teammate — standing-in-AoE is your default state; teammates are nearby.
  2. #16 FireRate Standing Still — persistent-zone spells benefit from standing in them.
  3. #7 CritDamage Standing Still — same standing-still trigger; double-dips.
  4. #15 ElementalDmg / Infusion — if you have multi-spell variety.

Boss Killer

  1. #7 CritDamage Standing Still — you’ll be kiting or anchored at boss range; static moments give the bonus.
  2. #14 CritDamage / Revive — pre-boss revives compound here.
  3. #15 ElementalDmg / Infusion — even if you’re not full Element Specialist, 2 infusions = +20% multiplier on every hit.
  4. #16 FireRate Standing Still — matters more for sustained DPS spells (Beam) than burst (Rifle).

Per-artifact notes (top 5 only — deep dives)

#15 IncreaseElementalDamagePerInfusionPerformed

SSS · ElementStrengthMultiplier

The only artifact that pushes the truly-multiplicative ElementStrengthMultiplier stat. +10 per infusion event means picking it after 2–3 infusions already pays back instantly, and every subsequent infusion event compounds. Pairs with Moon Mage (whose SpellsAndInfusionsGrantedEarlier unique increases total infusion count in a run).

#13 IncreaseDamagePerArmor

SSS · Damage from Armor

The single most influential build-defining artifact: it changes the value proposition of Armor from defence to offence. Parameters = [1, 0.25]; inferred meaning: add +1 Damage per N Armor where the rate is 0.25 per point — every 4 Armor = +1% Damage in the additive bracket. A Tank-DPS build at 100 Armor sits at +25% from this source alone.

#7 IncreaseCriticalDamageWhileStandingStill

S · CriticalDamage

+100 CritDamage conditional on standing still. Snapshot behaviour (see snapshot trap): casting before moving locks the bonus into the projectile’s DamageInfo. Cast then dodge is the play pattern. Worthless on Trail / Proximity users who never stop moving.

#2 AddArmorEqualToPartyLevel

S · Armor

No explicit parameters in the data; the IL2CPP class reads the sum of party-member levels each frame. Solo at level 30 = +30 Armor; 4-player party each at level 30 = +120 Armor. This is the single biggest Armor source in the game and the natural partner to #13.

#16 IncreaseFireRateWhileStandingStill

S · FireRate

+20 FireRate conditional on standing still. Pairs with #7 (same trigger) for the standing-still package on spells like Beam, Slash (with size-when-still variant), Bell, and BombLauncher. Multiplicative against your Damage bracket — this is high-leverage even at 0% existing FireRate modifiers.

Honourable mention: the heal-on-X family

#17 HealOnCrit (0.3 HP), #6 HealOnDodge (20 HP), #9 HealOnLevelUp (10 HP), #19 HealOnXP (15 HP / orb). These all look defensive but their real role is enabling: they let you take the Glass Cannon LessHP artifacts safely, or sustain through bullet-hell phases without committing to defensive stats. Stack one heal-on-X with one offence artifact; don’t stack two heal-on-X.
