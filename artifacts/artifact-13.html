Artifact 13 — Increase Damage Per Armor

ArtifactType enum: IncreaseDamagePerArmor (id 13). Affects Damage. Spawn rate 50.

Effect

Increase Damage Per Armor modifies the Damage stat. Continuous; reads current Armor each evaluation.

The core parameter is 1: Either an armor-cost step (1 armor required per increment) or a flat baseline factor for the damage formula.

Implemented by the compiled class . AddsModifier in the asset YAML is true; spawn rate 50.

Parameters

When to pick

  • Any armor-stacking build (Artifacts 2, 12, armor improvements).
  • Beware: a companion class reduces move-speed per armor — your kite suffers.
  • Damage scaling is generous on high-armor classes.

Synergizes with

  • Artifact 2 — Add Armor Equal To Level
    Pairs with Artifact 2 (AddArmorEqualToLevel) and Artifact 12 (IncreaseArmorPerObjectiveCompleted)

Other synergy hints

Pairs with Armor-granting spells/improvements

Avoid combining with

  • DecreaseMovementSpeedPerArmor exists in code — heavy armor may slow you.
  • Kite / dodge builds that need raw movement speed.
