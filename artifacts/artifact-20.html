Artifact 20 — Increase Crit Damage For Every Common

ArtifactType enum: IncreaseCritDamageForEveryCommon (id 20). Affects CriticalDamage. Spawn rate 100.

Pick it early

Spawn rate decays over time — this artifact appears more often in early stores and is rarer later.

Effect

Increase Crit Damage For Every Common modifies the CriticalDamage stat. Each time a store offers Common-rarity items, +1 per Common item shown.

The core parameter is 1: increasePerCommonStoreItemEncountered — flat CriticalDamage bonus added per Common-rarity store item encountered (likely +1 CritDamage stat point per Common item seen). Applied through the improvements manager, not as a modifier.

Implemented by the compiled class Artifact_IncreaseCriticalDamageOnCommonStoreItemEncountered. AddsModifier in the asset YAML is false; spawn rate 100 (decays over time).
