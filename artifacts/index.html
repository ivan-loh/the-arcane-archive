20 Artifacts — Passive Effects

Artifacts are run-altering passive items. Most use the computed modifier hierarchy — every game frame the artifact re-evaluates a small formula and pushes the resulting bonus into the player's stat pool (e.g. Armor or CriticalDamage). A handful (Heal On Crit, Heal On XP Pickup, Crit-Damage On Common) are event-driven instead — they react to gameplay events rather than adding a continuous stat modifier. Several artifacts only spawn in multiplayer. See Mechanics for the full pipeline.

#19

Heal On XP Pickup

Artifact_19 · spawn rate 50

Event-driven (no stat modifier) Healing Reactive

Event: each XP gem pickup rolls a 15% chance to heal for 1 HP.

  • Parameter[0] = 15 high
    chanceToHeal — percentage chance (15%) that an XP pickup triggers a heal. Field order in the class is 'chanceToHeal' (0x20) then 'healAmount' (0x24).
  • Parameter[1] = 1 high
    healAmount — heal applied when the chance roll succeeds (1 HP per qualifying XP pickup).

Synergies

Pairs with PickupRadius (StatType=11) and Luck — collect more XP, trigger more rolls Pairs with XPGain artifacts (Artifact 3) for compounding XP-driven sustain Weak heal magnitude; best as supplemental sustain layer

Source: Artifact_19.asset · Artifact_HealOnXpPickup (dump line 262514)
