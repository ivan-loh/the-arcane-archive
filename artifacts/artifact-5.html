Artifact 05 — Increase Damage For Every Nearby Teammate

ArtifactType enum: IncreaseDamageForEveryNearbyTeammate (id 5). Affects Damage. Spawn rate 50.

Co-op only

This artifact is hidden from solo runs (HideForSinglePlayer = true).

Effect

Increase Damage For Every Nearby Teammate modifies the Damage stat. Each ally within 10 units adds one stack of damage bonus.

The core parameter is 20: Bonus damage (likely +20% per nearby teammate within MaxDistanceForDamageIncrease=10 units). Class enumerates other GameplayPlayers and filters by distance < 10.

Implemented by the IL2CPP class ArtifactStatModifier_IncreaseDamagePerNearbyPartyMember. AddsModifier in the asset YAML is true; spawn rate 50.
