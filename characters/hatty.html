Playstyle & synergy

+6 Damage and +15 ElementWeaknessMultiplier on a 300-HP frame: Hatty melts elementally-vulnerable swarms but folds to a single mistake. Slot Smg and a defensive artifact early.

He needs the team to hold aggro. Pair with Smithy or Ludwig in co-op. Avoid solo Nightmare runs without lifesteal or dodge stacking.
