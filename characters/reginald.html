Playstyle & synergy

Reginald's +2 Luck and +10 Drop Chance lift artifact and rarity rolls all run long, so he benefits from any spell — the staple Orb kit pairs cleanly with rarity-scaling artifacts like Lucky Charm.

Look for artifacts that trigger on pickup or scale with drop volume. Reginald has no defensive identity, so prioritize an early Armor or Dodge upgrade in shops.
