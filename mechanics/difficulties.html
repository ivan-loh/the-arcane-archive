Difficulties

Three difficulty tiers gate run challenge level. Each tier is unlocked by clearing every world on the previous tier (see threat-level challenges). Difficulty multiplies enemy health and damage, increases elite spawn pressure, and is required to stack curses.

Scaling notes

Several enemy-event configurations expose per-difficulty parameters — for example the line-event gap size: 3.2 m on Normal, 2.5 m on Hard, 2.0 m on Nightmare; the bounce event spawns 9 / 12 / 14 enemies per drop respectively. Bring more clear and more movement on each step up.

Related

Curses Stack on top of difficulty for extra modifiers. Threat-level challenges Unlock Hard & Nightmare.
