Modifiers

Three opt-in run modifiers (the “Covenants”). Each is unlocked via a covenant challenge and changes a core rule of the run for the entire team.

3 modifiers Team-leader controlled

Peaceful Mode

modifier_1_peaceful_mode · modifier_id 0 · 1 stack

Co-op-team modifier. Toggled by the team leader; cannot be combined freely with other modifiers. See covenant challenges for unlock conditions.

Endless Mode

modifier_2_endless_mode · modifier_id 1 · 1 stack

Co-op-team modifier. Toggled by the team leader; cannot be combined freely with other modifiers. See covenant challenges for unlock conditions.

One Less Spell Mode

modifier_3_onelessspell_mode · modifier_id 2 · 3 stacks

Co-op-team modifier. Toggled by the team leader; cannot be combined freely with other modifiers. See covenant challenges for unlock conditions.
