Objectives

Ten optional mid-run objectives may roll during any wave. Completing them grants extra rewards and counts toward objective challenges.

Brief reference

  • Capture — hold a control zone while waves spawn against it.
  • Sacrifice — trade HP, gold or an item to clear an objective rune.
  • End Boss — the world boss; effectively a marker objective.
  • Collect — gather wave-spawned pickups within a time window.
  • Hunt — track and kill a marked target enemy.
  • Petrified Enemies — break a number of petrified statues before they un-freeze.
  • Clean — remove residue/effect tiles from the field.
  • Push — escort a moving target through enemies.
  • Order — kill targets in a specified sequence.
  • Herding — drive a group of mobs into a kill-zone.
