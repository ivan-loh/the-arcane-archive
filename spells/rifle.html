Synergies

  • AimAtHighestHP variant — Always shoots the most valuable target — perfect for elites/bosses.
  • Critical-damage artifacts — Single high-damage shots benefit most from crit scaling.
  • Range improvements — Rifle outranges most enemies; more Range = safer shots.
