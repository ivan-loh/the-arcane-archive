Bosses

The Spell Brigade ships 3 boss encounters, one per non-Astral world. Each gates the world's 20-wave run with a multi-phase fight, ~165–185k HP, and a 350-damage contact hit. All three scale with party size (scales_with_players = 1) and have 50 % slow resistance — Ice CC is half-effective against them.

Strategy quick reference

Universal rules

All three bosses share 350 contact damage, 50 % slow resistance, and scale with party size. Stack movement-speed artifacts, run at least one piercing damage source, and bring a spell that hits while you reposition (Beam, Bell, Aura).

Squid — bullet-hell dance Phase 3 reaches 24 projectile waves at 5.5 m/s. Pierce and pre-aim. Hydra — add control Healer adds keep her in stalemate. Bring AoE swap-targets. Toad — arena denial The puddle takes ~40 % of arena floor. Plan exits early.
Do not

Do not engage any boss without a movement spell or dash. The 350 contact damage will one-shot most builds at the moment of phase transition when your cooldowns are spent.
