What’s in the wiki

Every entity in the current build of The Spell Brigade has its own page. Each spell page covers base stats, element infusions, gameplay variants, and improvements. Each artifact page shows the scaling stat and its synergistic spells and heroes. Mechanics pages explain the underlying systems — the damage formula, the element matchup matrix, stat stacking, curses, and rarity tiers — and they are deeply cross-linked so you can dig from any spell to the math and back.

Methodology

All data on this wiki was extracted from the game’s shipped files (distributed game build) using the extraction pipeline and the extraction pipeline. The wiki is not affiliated with or endorsed by the developer. Content is text-only — no game art, audio, or logos — to stay on the safe side of copyright. Localized strings (item names, flavor text) are shown as Unity localization keys (e.g. SpellName_Arrow) when the strings themselves could not be unpacked from the compressed addressable bundles.
