Elite enemies

The bestiary lists four enemies with the elite behavior tag (the three explicit Elite* reskins plus the Hoarder loot piñata, which we group here for clarity). Each world reserves 4 elite spawn slots per run; the elite roster is shared, so picking the right counters is what carries you from wave 15 onward.

Elite-killing strategy

Elites all share scales_with_players = 1, meaning a full 4-player lobby effectively quadruples their HP pool. Plan for a dedicated elite-killer slot in your party comp.

Pro tip

The HealOnCrit family of artifacts turns elite fights into sustained-DPS exchanges; pair with a high-crit spell like Glacial Shard for free leech.

Warning

The Elite Buffer radiates a friendly damage aura. Killing it before it links with a Tripod/Snipjaw pack can be the difference between a one-clip wave and a wipe.
