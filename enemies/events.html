Enemy events

Beyond regular wave spawns, the game schedules 4 scripted enemy events — Bounce, Convergent, Line, and Mob — defined in EnemyEvent assets and triggered from data/meta.json > in_run_events. They are positional hazards, not stat-stick threats: most are killable, but the right answer is usually to move.

Events ramp with the Stronger Enemies curse stack and currently appear exclusively on Hard difficulty and above (most density on Astral Planes wave 1–18).

Bounce Event

Event type bounce · enemy_type_id 22 · enemy_event_id 3

Trigger
Hard difficulty, every other wave on Astral Planes (2,4,6,8,10,12,16,18) plus extra stacks with the Stronger Enemies curse.
Behavior
A projectile-like hazard bounces around the arena along a deterministic path. Lifespan ~10 seconds. Deals 0 contact damage by design — it is a positioning hazard, not a DPS source. 600 HP, can be destroyed.

How to handle

Predict the bounce arc and step through it rather than chase. Drop a Bell on the predicted next bounce site to delete it on contact.

Convergent Event

Event type convergent · enemy_type_id 18 · enemy_event_id n/a

Trigger
Hard / Cursed difficulty in late waves.
Behavior
Multiple enemies spawn at arena edge and converge on the player's current position. Each unit has 500 HP and 200 contact damage. The spawn ignores normal probability tables.

How to survive

Move perpendicular to the convergence vector and trail a damage aura (Venom Mist) — they self-funnel through it. Do not stand still to cast.
