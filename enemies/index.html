Bestiary

Every creature that can spawn in a run — pulled from the live EnemyPrefabRegistry. The Spell Brigade ships 36 enemy types: regular threats that build up wave density, elite variants with inflated health, scripted enemy events that change the arena, and three boss encounters that gate a world.

Behavior tags

Every enemy carries one or more behavior tags. Filter the regular and elite tables by clicking a chip.

melee ranged explosive support spawner ambush fast flying elite event boss objective
Tip

The fast tag denotes wave-21+ palette swaps with roughly +20–40 % movement speed but identical health. They are not a new mechanic — same enemy, faster intercepts.

Notable threats

Three enemies (all bosses) deserve a dedicated pre-run plan.

Boss

Squid

3 phases · Scorched Abyss

boss bullet-hell homing

The default boss and the only encounter with explicit bullet-hell phases. Phase 3 fires six concentric rotating walls per cycle — pierce or perish.

boss leap AoE

The tutorial boss; slowest movement of the three but leaves a Enemy_ToadDeadSplash area-of-denial puddle on phase transition.

Do not

Do not stand still during any boss bullet-hell phase, and do not chase Hydra past her healers — every healer left alive negates roughly 1.5 % of her max HP per second.
