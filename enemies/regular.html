Regular enemies

Of the 36 entries in the enemy registry, 25 are regular threats — the everyday wave fodder you see between elite spawns and boss phases. The remaining slots belong to 4 elites, 4 scripted events, and 3 bosses. Every stat below is pulled from the prefab definition in EnemyHealthContainer.maxHealth, Enemy.contactDamage, and the navigator's NavMeshAgent.m_Speed.

Tip

Wave spawn probability is relative, not absolute. A Crabling at 80.0 in wave 1 is the only thing that spawns; a Tripod at 40.0 in wave 10 competes against six other types.
