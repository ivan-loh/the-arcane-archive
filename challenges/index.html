Challenges

147 player-facing challenges across 16 categories. Most reward a Steam achievement, a cosmetic skin, an unlock, or a Title. Categories map to the in-game tracking tabs.

Categories

Boss

3 challenges

Kill each named boss once. Three boss-slay challenges — one per boss currently in game.

Covenant

3 challenges

Unlock the three Covenant modifiers (Peaceful, Endless, OneLessSpell) by clearing specific milestones.

Curse

16 challenges

Earn the seven curse stacks. Most require clearing a world on Nightmare with a minimum devotion count.

Element

7 challenges

Track cumulative damage dealt with each element family. Goal numbers are large — thousands to millions.

Enchantment

6 challenges

Unlock upgrade tiers and final upgrades through progression milestones.
