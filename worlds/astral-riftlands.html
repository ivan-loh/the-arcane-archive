Astral Planes

World 4 Boss: (endgame boss) 20 waves 20-minute run

Reality thins. Stars wheel underfoot and laws of physics negotiate. The Astral Planes is the hardest currently released world — an endgame gauntlet against celestial swarms and an unrevealed final entity. Only veteran brigades cross this threshold.

Otherworldly astral realm with celestial enemies. The hardest currently released world.

Boss

The run culminates at wave 20 in a fight against (endgame boss). Boss arrival is the run's hard checkpoint — survive the wave and the boss in the run-duration window (20 minutes) to complete the world.

Elite enemies & events

Like every world, Astral Planes spawns 4 elites per run. The elite roster is shared across all worlds — EliteBuffer, Spawner, EliteExplodingTick, EliteTeethyWorm — selected at random at 14:00 in-run. Curse stacks add up to three extra elite spawns at 10:20, 16:20 and 4:10.

Five enemy-event configurations may trigger during a run (Bounce, Convergent, Line, Mob, etc.). See enemy events for the spawn patterns.

Objectives

Optional mid-run objectives may roll for any world. The pool: Capture · Clean · Collect · End Boss · Herding · Hunt · Order · Petrified Enemies · Push · Sacrifice.

Recommended strategy

Treat the Astral Planes as a stat-check on your meta-progression. Without near-maxed upgrade tiers and a Legendary artifact or two, even the early waves overwhelm. Wave 5, 10 and 15 astral overrides crank concurrent enemy counts higher than any other world — bring a build that snowballs on kill-feedback (XPGain, GoldGain, Luck) and never stops moving.

Tip

Bring at least one source of area-clear and one source of sustained single-target damage. The boss demands the latter; the swarm waves demand the former.
