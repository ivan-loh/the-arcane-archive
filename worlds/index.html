Worlds

Four playable worlds form the core campaign of The Spell Brigade. Each is a fixed-length run (20 minutes / 20 waves) ending in a unique boss. Worlds reuse a shared wave timeline but apply world-specific spawn overrides at waves 4, 6, 9, 12, 14, 15 and 18 to shift their enemy mix.

Verdant Meadows

World 1 · Boss: Toad

20 waves 4 elites 20-min run demo

Sunlit grasslands with rolling hills, mushrooms and toad-themed enemies. The starting world.

Crystal Frostlands

World 2 · Boss: Hydra

20 waves 4 elites 20-min run

Frozen ancient ruins of ice and crystal. Slowing terrain and a multi-headed hydra boss.

Scorched Abyss

World 3 · Boss: Squid

20 waves 4 elites 20-min run

Volcanic pit of fire, lava and ash. Squid boss rises from molten depths.

Astral Planes

World 4 · Boss: (endgame boss)

20 waves 4 elites 20-min run

Otherworldly astral realm with celestial enemies. The hardest currently released world.
